Currently Tree House requires pilot and map assets from Zook Yard 2 to be present to load correctly.

Tree house has new DM strategies incorporated, one is the 4 radar stations that enhance players radar range once they take possession of them by hopping into them briefly. 

Aquirring pods at the Temples of Pod requires standing on the altar of holy algae and waiting for them to be supplied. Hopefully you wont be sniped while waiting.

There are hidden elevators as well as the more obvious ones.
